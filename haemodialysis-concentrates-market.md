<p><p>The haemodialysis concentrates market is primarily divided into bicarbonate and acid concentrates, both essential for the dialysis process. Bicarbonate concentrates provide the necessary alkalinity to the dialysate, helping to maintain acid-base balance in patients. Conversely, acid concentrates supply essential electrolytes and help to manage pH levels. These solutions are crucial for effective waste removal from blood and are essential for the treatment of patients with renal failure, contributing to patient safety and treatment efficacy in haemodialysis.</p></p>
