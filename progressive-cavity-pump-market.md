<p><p>The progressive cavity pump market is segmented into three main types: single screw pumps, double screw pumps, and three screw pumps. Single screw pumps use a helical rotor within a stator to transport fluids, offering efficiency and gentle handling of shear-sensitive materials. Double screw pumps utilize two screws for balanced fluid movement, ideal for high-viscosity applications. Three screw pumps, featuring three intermeshing screws, provide high flow rates and pressure, suitable for various industrial applications, including oil and gas, food processing, and wastewater management.</p></p>
