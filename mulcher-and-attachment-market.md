Fixed teeth rotors utilize solid, stationary blades designed for efficient material processing, ideal for tough vegetation and dense brush. Conversely, swinging hammer rotors employ movable hammers that can adapt to varying material densities, providing a more flexible and aggressive cutting approach. These market segments cater to different agricultural, industrial, and landscaping needs, enhancing versatility and effectiveness in land management and vegetation control.</p></p>
