<p><p>Zinc-air batteries come in three main market types. Primary (non-rechargeable) batteries are designed for single use, offering high energy density for devices like hearing aids. Secondary (rechargeable) batteries can be cycled multiple times, suitable for applications requiring frequent recharging, like electric vehicles. The mechanical recharge market focuses on systems that convert mechanical energy into electrical energy, facilitating recharging through movement, which can enhance sustainability and reduce reliance on traditional charging methods in various applications.</p></p>
