<p><p>The IAQ (Indoor Air Quality) Monitor market comprises two main types: fixed and portable monitors. Fixed IAQ monitors are installed in specific locations, providing continuous, real-time data on air quality indicators like particulate matter and volatile organic compounds. They are ideal for permanent setups in offices or homes. Portable IAQ monitors, on the other hand, offer flexibility and convenience, allowing users to measure air quality in various locations. These devices are useful for assessing air conditions on the go, such as in vehicles or different rooms.</p></p>
