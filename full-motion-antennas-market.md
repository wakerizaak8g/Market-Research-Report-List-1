<p><strong>The Full Motion Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Full Motion Antennas are categorized into active and passive types based on their operational mechanisms. Active antennas incorporate electronic systems to enhance signal reception and transmission, providing superior performance in dynamic environments. They often include motors for real-time alignment with target signals. In contrast, passive antennas rely solely on their physical design and do not have active components, making them simpler and more cost-effective but less adaptable to changing signal directions. Both types serve distinct applications in telecommunications and broadcasting.</p></p>
