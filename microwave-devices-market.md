<p><ul><li>Active Microwave Devices</li><li>Passive Microwave Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microwave devices market is segmented into active and passive types. Active microwave devices generate and amplify microwave signals, including components like transistors, oscillators, and amplifiers, essential for communication and radar systems. In contrast, passive microwave devices do not generate signals but manipulate them, such as filters, couplers, and antennas. Both types are crucial for various applications, including telecommunications, aerospace, and industrial processes, driving innovation and growth in the microwave technology landscape.</p></p>
