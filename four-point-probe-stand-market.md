<p><p>The Four Point Probe Stand Market consists of two primary cooling method types: LHe/LN2 and Closed Cycle (cryogen-free). The LHe/LN2 method utilizes liquid helium or nitrogen for effective cooling, enhancing measurement precision for resistivity in semiconductors and materials at low temperatures. Conversely, the Closed Cycle method employs a cryocooler, eliminating the need for liquid cryogens, making it more environmentally friendly and cost-effective. Both methods cater to diverse research and industrial applications, ensuring optimal thermal conditions for accurate probe measurements.</p></p>
