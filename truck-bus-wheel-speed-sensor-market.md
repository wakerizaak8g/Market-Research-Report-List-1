<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Truck & Bus Wheel Speed Sensor market comprises primarily two types: Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize a magnetic field to produce an electrical signal correlated with wheel speed, offering high precision and reliability. In contrast, Magnetic Electric sensors detect changes in magnetic fields generated by rotating components, converting this data into electrical signals to gauge wheel speed. Both types play crucial roles in vehicle stability, anti-lock braking systems, and overall safety in commercial transportation.</p></p>
