<p><p>The Amplified Piezoelectric Actuator market features two primary types: stack actuators and stripe actuators. Stack actuators consist of layered piezoelectric materials that expand and contract with applied voltage, offering high force and precision in compact designs. Stripe actuators, on the other hand, utilize a series of stripes to provide linear motion, typically favored for applications requiring longer stroke lengths and lower force. Both types are essential in various industries, including robotics, aerospace, and medical devices, for precise control and movement.</p></p>
