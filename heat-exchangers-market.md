<p><p>Heat exchangers are critical components in various industries for transferring heat between fluids. The shell and tube type features a series of tubes where one fluid flows through the tubes while another passes around them, providing efficient heat transfer and high pressure resistance, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications. Plate and frame heat exchangers consist of multiple plates stacked together, offering a compact design and increased surface area for effective heat transfer, suitable for lower pressure applications and easy maintenance. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
