<p><p>The SUV and Pickup Wheel Speed Sensor market is primarily segmented into Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type sensors. Hall Type sensors utilize a semiconductor to detect changes in magnetic fields, providing precise wheel speed data, ideal for modern vehicles with advanced traction control systems. In contrast, Magnetic Electric Type sensors generate an electrical signal through the movement of a magnetic field, commonly used in older vehicle models. Both types play crucial roles in vehicle stability, safety, and performance monitoring.</p></p>
