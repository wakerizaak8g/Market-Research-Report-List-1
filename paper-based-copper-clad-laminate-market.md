<p><p>The paper-based copper clad laminate market consists of two primary types: flame retardant and non-flame retardant laminates. Flame retardant paper-based copper clad laminates are designed to resist ignition and slow the spread of flame, making them suitable for applications requiring enhanced safety. In contrast, non-flame retardant laminates lack these fire-resistant properties and are often used in less hazardous environments. Both types serve essential roles in the electronics industry, providing insulation and support for printed circuit boards.</p></p>
