<p><p>The head gasket market consists of various types, each with distinct properties and applications. Multi-Layer Steel (MLS) gaskets offer durability and resistance to high temperatures, making them suitable for performance engines. Solid Copper gaskets provide excellent thermal conductivity and are often used in racing applications. Composite gaskets, made from materials like fibers and rubber, offer a balance between sealing performance and cost-effectiveness. Elastomeric gaskets, typically made from synthetic materials, provide flexibility and better sealing in low-pressure applications.</p></p>
