<p><strong>The Deicing Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Regulating Deicing Systems</li><li>Constant Wattage Deicing Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The deicing cable market primarily consists of two types: self-regulating deicing systems and constant wattage deicing systems. Self-regulating systems automatically adjust their heat output based on temperature changes, providing efficient energy use and preventing overheating. In contrast, constant wattage systems deliver a fixed amount of heat along their length, ensuring consistent warmth but with a higher risk of overheating in certain conditions. Both systems are widely used in residential and commercial applications to maintain safe, ice-free surfaces.</p></p>
