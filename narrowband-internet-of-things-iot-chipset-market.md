<p><strong>The Narrowband Internet-of-Things (IoT) Chipset Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stand-Alone</li><li>Guard Band</li><li>In-Band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Narrowband Internet-of-Things (IoT) chipset market is categorized into three types: Stand-Alone, Guard Band, and In-Band. </p><p>Stand-Alone chipsets operate independently in dedicated frequency bands, ensuring maximum performance and minimal interference. Guard Band chipsets utilize a small frequency range between two larger bands, enhancing communication efficiency while reducing disruption. In-Band chipsets function within existing cellular networks, allowing IoT devices to relay data alongside traditional signals, thus optimizing spectrum use and enabling seamless integration with existing infrastructure.</p></p>
