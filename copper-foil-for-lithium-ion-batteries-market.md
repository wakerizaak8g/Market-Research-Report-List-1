<p><p>The Copper Foil for Lithium-ion Batteries market is primarily segmented into two types: Electrolytic Copper Foil and Rolled Copper Foil. </p><p>Electrolytic Copper Foil is produced through an electroplating process, resulting in a thin, flexible foil that offers uniform thickness and high conductivity, making it ideal for battery applications. </p><p>In contrast, Rolled Copper Foil is manufactured by physically rolling large copper sheets, leading to thicker and less pliable foil. Each type serves distinct requirements in battery performance and application efficiency, catering to varying industry needs.</p></p>
