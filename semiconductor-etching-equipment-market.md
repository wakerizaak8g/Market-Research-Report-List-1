<p><ul><li>Dry Etching Equipment</li><li>Wet Etching Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor etching equipment market is primarily divided into two types: dry etching equipment and wet etching equipment. Dry etching employs gases to remove material from the wafer surface, offering precision and control for intricate patterns, making it ideal for advanced semiconductor devices. Conversely, wet etching utilizes chemical solutions to dissolve materials, providing a simpler and cost-effective method for larger scale applications. Each type serves distinct purposes in the semiconductor manufacturing process, catering to varying technological and production needs.</p></p>
