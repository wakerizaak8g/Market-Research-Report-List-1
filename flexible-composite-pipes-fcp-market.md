<p><p>Flexible Composite Pipes (FCP) are advanced piping systems designed to accommodate various fluid transport needs. The market for FCP is divided into two main types: Non-metallic Flexible Composite Pipes and Metallic Flexible Composite Pipes. Non-metallic FCPs are typically lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and suitable for aggressive environments, making them ideal for the oil and gas industry. In contrast, Metallic FCPs combine strength with flexibility, offering superior pressure handling and durability, making them suitable for high-stress applications across various sectors.</p></p>
