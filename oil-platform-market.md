<p><strong>The Oil Platform Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Platforms</li><li>Semi-submersible Platforms</li><li>Floating Platforms</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil platform market consists of several types: </p><p>1. **Fixed Platforms** are anchored directly to the ocean floor, providing stability for shallow waters. </p><p>2. **Semi-submersible Platforms** float on the water's surface, stabilized by submerged columns, ideal for deeper offshore drilling. </p><p>3. **Floating Platforms** are fully buoyant structures, capable of mobile operations across various depths. </p><p>4. **Others** include various specialized platforms like Tension-leg platforms and Spar platforms, designed for specific needs and environments, expanding exploration capabilities.</p></p>
