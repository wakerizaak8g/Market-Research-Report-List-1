<p><p>Injection molding equipment is categorized into vertical and horizontal types. Vertical injection molding machines operate with the mold positioned vertically, allowing for better space utilization and easy automation, making them ideal for small, intricate parts. Horizontal machines feature a horizontal mold alignment, which is more common for larger parts and high-volume production. This type allows for faster cycle times and better material flow. Each type serves specific manufacturing needs, influencing production efficiency and part complexity.</p></p>
