<p><ul><li>Endometrial Ablation</li><li>MRI Guided Procedures</li><li>Hysterectomy</li><li>Myomectomy</li><li>Uterine Artery Embolization</li><li>Radiofrequency Ablation</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Uterine fibroids treatment encompasses various procedures. Endometrial ablation involves destroying the uterine lining to reduce bleeding. MRI-guided procedures offer precise targeting of fibroids using imaging. Hysterectomy entails the complete removal of the uterus, while myomectomy focuses on removing only the fibroids. Uterine artery embolization blocks blood flow to fibroids, causing them to shrink. Radiofrequency ablation uses heat to target and destroy fibroid tissue. Other treatments may include medication or less common techniques. Each option varies in invasiveness and recovery time.</p></p>
