<p><strong>The Surgical Skin Closure Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closure Strips</li><li>Tissue Adhesive</li><li>Sutures</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surgical skin closure device market comprises three main types: closure strips, tissue adhesives, and sutures. Closure strips are adhesive strips used to hold together skin edges, ideal for small wounds. Tissue adhesives are medical-grade glues that bond skin surfaces, offering a quick and painless option for wound closure. Sutures are traditional stitched closures, providing strong support for deeper or larger wounds. Each method has specific applications, advantages, and characteristics suited to varying surgical needs and wound types.</p></p>
