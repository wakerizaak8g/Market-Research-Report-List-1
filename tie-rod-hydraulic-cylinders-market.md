<p><p>The tie rod hydraulic cylinder market is primarily categorized into two types: single-acting and double-acting cylinders. Single-acting cylinders employ hydraulic fluid to extend the piston, relying on external forces or springs for retraction, making them suitable for applications with limited space. In contrast, double-acting cylinders utilize hydraulic pressure for both extension and retraction, offering greater control and efficiency in various industrial operations. This differentiation allows manufacturers to better meet specific application requirements across different sectors.</p></p>
