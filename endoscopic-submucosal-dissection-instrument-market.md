<p><ul><li>Electrosurgical Knives</li><li>Injection Needles</li><li>Hemostatic Forceps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Endoscopic Submucosal Dissection (ESD) instrument market comprises three main types: electrosurgical knives, injection needles, and hemostatic forceps. Electrosurgical knives are used for precise cutting and coagulation during dissection, enhancing visibility and minimizing bleeding. Injection needles facilitate the submucosal injection of saline or other solutions, creating a space for easier access to lesions. Hemostatic forceps are employed to control bleeding by clamping blood vessels, ensuring a safer procedure overall. Together, these instruments enhance the efficacy and safety of ESD procedures.</p></p>
