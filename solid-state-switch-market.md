<p><p>The solid-state switch market encompasses various types of Solid-State Relays (SSRs) categorized by their output configurations. AC Output SSRs are designed to control alternating current loads, providing reliable switching without mechanical wear. DC Output SSRs focus on direct current applications, ensuring efficient switching in low-voltage environments. AC/DC Output SSRs are versatile solutions capable of handling both AC and DC loads, catering to diverse industrial applications. Each type offers advantages like faster switching and longer lifespan compared to electromechanical relays.</p></p>
