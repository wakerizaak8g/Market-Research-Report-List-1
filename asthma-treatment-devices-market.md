<p><p>The asthma treatment devices market primarily comprises nebulizers and inhalers. Nebulizers convert liquid medication into a fine mist, making it easier for patients to inhale, often used in more severe cases or for young children. Inhalers are handheld devices that deliver medication directly to the lungs, available in two main types: metered-dose inhalers (MDIs) and dry powder inhalers (DPIs). Both devices are essential in managing asthma symptoms, enabling effective medication delivery to improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
