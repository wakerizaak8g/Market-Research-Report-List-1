<p><p>The Ready-to-Use Therapeutic Food (RUTF) market encompasses three primary types: solid, paste, and drinkable formulations. Solid RUTF often comes in bar or cookie form, providing a convenient nutritional snack. Paste RUTF is a nutrient-dense, spreadable option that supports easy consumption for malnourished individuals, especially children. Drinkable RUTF offers a liquid format that promotes hydration alongside nutrition, making it accessible for those who may struggle with solid foods. Each type serves distinct needs in treating malnutrition.</p></p>
