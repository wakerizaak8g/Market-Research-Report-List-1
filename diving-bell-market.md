<p><strong>The Diving Bell Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Bell</li><li>Closed Bell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diving bell market types include wet bells and closed bells, each serving distinct underwater operations. Wet bells are open systems that allow divers to breathe surface-supplied air while submerged, facilitating quick access to the surface. In contrast, closed bells are pressurized units where divers work in a mixed gas environment, providing protection against high pressures and enhancing safety for extended deep-sea operations. Both systems are essential in commercial diving, offshore construction, and underwater exploration, catering to specific operational demands.</p></p>
