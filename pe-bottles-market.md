<p><p>The PE bottles market encompasses three primary types: HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene), LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), and MDPE (Medium-Density Polyethylene). HDPE is known for its strength and durability, making it ideal for containers and industrial applications. LDPE is more flexible and is commonly used for squeeze bottles and films. MDPE strikes a balance between the two, offering moderate strength and flexibility. Each type serves distinct purposes in packaging, driven by their unique physical properties and applications across various industries.</p></p>
