<p><p>The H. pylori test kit market primarily comprises two types: antibody tests and antigen tests. Antibody tests detect the presence of antibodies produced in response to H. pylori infection, indicating past or current infection. These tests are typically done using blood samples. In contrast, antigen tests identify specific H. pylori proteins in stool samples, providing a more direct indication of an active infection. Both testing methods play crucial roles in diagnosing and managing gastrointestinal disorders related to H. pylori.</p></p>
