<p><strong>The Steam Trap Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermodynamic</li><li>Mechanical</li><li>Thermostatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam trap market is categorized into three main types: thermodynamic, mechanical, and thermostatic. Thermodynamic steam traps use the principles of thermodynamics to discharge condensate and air, typically featuring a disc that opens and closes based on pressure changes. Mechanical steam traps operate through float mechanisms that open or close based on condensate levels. Thermostatic steam traps utilize temperature-sensitive elements to regulate condensate removal, effectively responding to temperature variations. Each type serves unique applications in steam systems for optimal efficiency.</p></p>
