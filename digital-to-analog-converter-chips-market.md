<p><p>The digital-to-analog converter (DAC) chips market encompasses several types, each serving distinct applications. Flash DACs offer rapid conversion speeds ideal for high-frequency applications. Folding DACs balance speed and resolution for more efficient designs. Successive Approximation Register (SAR) DACs provide a compromise between speed and complexity, making them suitable for mid-range applications. Pipelined DACs enable high throughput and resolution, catering to applications like audio and video processing. Additionally, other types may include hybrid and sigma-delta DACs, addressing diverse requirements.</p></p>
