<p><strong>The Silicon Based Fingerprint Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Swipe Sensors</li><li>Area Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silicon Based Fingerprint Sensors market is categorized into two main types: swipe sensors and area sensors. Swipe sensors capture fingerprints by having users swipe their finger across a sensing area, suitable for mobile devices and access control systems. In contrast, area sensors capture the entire fingerprint at once, providing higher resolution and detail, ideal for secure environments like banking and law enforcement. Both types offer unique advantages in terms of convenience, security, and application suitability.</p></p>
