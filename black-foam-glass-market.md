<p><strong>The Black Foam Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Cell</li><li>Open Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The black foam glass market is segmented into two main types: closed cell and open cell. Closed cell foam glass features tightly packed cells that provide excellent insulation and moisture resistance, making it ideal for applications in construction and refrigeration. In contrast, open cell foam glass has a more porous structure, allowing for better sound absorption and airflow, suitable for acoustic insulation and lightweight applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including building, automotive, and HVAC, based on their unique properties.</p></p>
