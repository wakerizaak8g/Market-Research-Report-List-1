<p><p>The Medium Radar System market is primarily categorized into two types: Continuous Wave (CW) Radars and Pulsed Radars. CW Radars transmit a continuous signal, allowing for accurate velocity detection, making them ideal for applications like speed monitoring and tracking. Conversely, Pulsed Radars send short bursts of energy, enabling long-range detection and high-resolution imaging, suitable for weather monitoring and surveillance. Both types play crucial roles in aerospace, defense, and transportation sectors, catering to diverse operational requirements.</p></p>
