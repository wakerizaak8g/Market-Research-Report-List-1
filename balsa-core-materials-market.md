<p><p>The Balsa Core Materials market can be categorized into two main types: monolayer and multilayer. Monolayer balsa cores consist of a single layer of balsa wood, offering lightweight and excellent strength-to-weight properties, making them suitable for applications where simplicity and efficiency are key. In contrast, multilayer balsa cores are composed of multiple layers, enhancing mechanical properties and providing improved resistance to compression and shear forces. This makes multilayer options ideal for more demanding applications, such as aerospace and marine industries.</p></p>
