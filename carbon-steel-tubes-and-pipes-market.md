<p><strong>The Carbon Steel Tubes and Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Welded Tubes and Pipes</li><li>Seamless Tubes and Pipes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon steel tubes and pipes market consists mainly of two types: welded and seamless. Welded tubes and pipes are manufactured by joining steel strips or plates together, making them more economical and suitable for various applications. Seamless tubes and pipes, on the other hand, are produced from solid steel billets, offering higher strength and durability without any weld seams. Each type serves different industrial requirements, with seamless options preferred for high-pressure environments and welded varieties favored for their cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
