<p><p>The Wireless Water Leak Detectors market is categorized into two main types: passive and active leak detectors. Passive leak detectors use sensors that rely on the presence of water to trigger an alarm, functioning effectively with minimal energy consumption. In contrast, active leak detectors proactively monitor moisture levels and can actively send alerts through wireless systems when leaks are detected. Both types enhance water conservation efforts and property protection by providing timely notifications, but they differ in their operational approach and energy needs.</p></p>
