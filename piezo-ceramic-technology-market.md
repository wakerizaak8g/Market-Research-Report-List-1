<p><p>The piezo ceramic technology market comprises several key types, including Lead Zinc Titanates (PZT), Lead Titanate (PT), and Lead Magnesium Niobate (PMN), among others. PZT is widely used for its strong piezoelectric properties, making it ideal for sensors and actuators. Lead Titanate offers high electromechanical coupling but with a lower temperature stability. PMN is known for its high sensitivity and is often utilized in complex applications like medical imaging. Other materials include variations that cater to specific industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
