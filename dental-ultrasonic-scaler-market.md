<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dental Ultrasonic Scaler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive Ultrasonic Scalers</li><li>Piezoelectric Ultrasonic Scalers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental ultrasonic scaler market is primarily divided into two types: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric ultrasonic scalers. Magnetostrictive ultrasonic scalers utilize a magnetic field to vibrate a stack of metal strips, producing oscillations for effective plaque removal. In contrast, piezoelectric ultrasonic scalers employ ceramic crystals that expand and contract with an electric current, allowing for precise scaling with minimal tissue trauma. Both types enhance dental hygiene procedures by improving efficiency and providing more comfortable experiences for patients.</p></p>
