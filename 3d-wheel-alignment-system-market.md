<p><p>The 3D Wheel Alignment System market features two primary types: imaging wheel alignment machines and diagnostic wheel alignment machines. Imaging wheel alignment machines utilize advanced cameras and sensors to capture precise three-dimensional measurements of a vehicle's wheel angles, providing quick and accurate alignments. In contrast, diagnostic wheel alignment machines focus on analyzing existing wheel alignment conditions and pinpointing issues using specialized software and tools, helping technicians make informed adjustments to enhance vehicle performance and safety.</p></p>
