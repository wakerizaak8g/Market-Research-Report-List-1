<p><ul><li>Wi-Fi-Enabled Smart Coffee Maker</li><li>Bluetooth-Enabled Smart Coffee Maker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Coffee Maker market is primarily divided into two types: Wi-Fi-enabled and Bluetooth-enabled models. Wi-Fi-enabled smart coffee makers connect to home networks, allowing users to control brewing remotely via smartphone apps, schedule brews, and integrate with smart home systems. In contrast, Bluetooth-enabled smart coffee makers connect directly to devices like smartphones for local control, offering features like brew customization and status updates but lacking the broader connectivity of Wi-Fi models. Both enhance convenience and user experience in coffee preparation.</p></p>
