<p><p>The axial expansion joints market is primarily categorized into flange type and welding type joints. Flange type joints are characterized by their bolted connections, allowing for easier installation and maintenance, making them suitable for various applications. Welding type joints, on the other hand, involve direct welding to pipes, providing a robust and leak-proof connection ideal for high-pressure systems. Both types are essential for accommodating thermal expansion and contraction in piping systems, ensuring structural integrity and longevity.</p></p>
