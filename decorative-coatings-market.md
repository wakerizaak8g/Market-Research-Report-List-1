<p><ul><li>Primer</li><li>Enamel</li><li>Emulsions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The decorative coatings market comprises three primary types: primers, enamels, and emulsions. Primers are preparatory coatings that enhance adhesion and durability of topcoats, often sealing surfaces. Enamels provide a hard, glossy finish suitable for both interior and exterior applications, known for their durability and aesthetic appeal. Emulsions, water-based coatings, are popular for their ease of application and low environmental impact, often used on walls and ceilings, providing a smooth finish and extensive color options for residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
