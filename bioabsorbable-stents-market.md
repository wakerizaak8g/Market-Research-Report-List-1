<p><ul><li>Peripheral Bioabsorbable Stents</li><li>Coronary Bioabsorbable Stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bioabsorbable stents market is divided into two primary types: peripheral bioabsorbable stents and coronary bioabsorbable stents. Peripheral bioabsorbable stents are designed for treating vascular lesions in peripheral arteries, enhancing blood flow while gradually dissolving over time. In contrast, coronary bioabsorbable stents target coronary artery disease, supporting the artery during healing and then dissolving to restore natural blood flow without long-term metallic implants. Both types aim to improve patient outcomes, reduce complications, and promote better vascular health.</p></p>
