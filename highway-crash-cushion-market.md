<p><strong>The Highway Crash Cushion Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-redirective Crash Cushions</li><li>Redirective Crash Cushions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The highway crash cushion market comprises two main types: non-redirective and redirective crash cushions. Non-redirective crash cushions are designed to absorb energy and mitigate the impact of vehicle collisions without redirecting them to another path, minimizing the risk of secondary accidents. In contrast, redirective crash cushions aim to guide errant vehicles back onto the roadway after impact, enhancing safety by preventing vehicles from veering into hazardous areas. Both types are essential for improving road safety and reducing collision severity.</p></p>
