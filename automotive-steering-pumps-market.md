<p><strong>The Automotive Steering Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Hydraulic Power Steering</li><li>Electronic Hydraulic Power Steering</li><li>Electric Power Steering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive steering pump market comprises three main types: Mechanical Hydraulic Power Steering (MHPS), which relies on a belt-driven pump for hydraulic assistance; Electronic Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS), which combines hydraulic systems with electronic control for improved responsiveness; and Electric Power Steering (EPS), which uses electric motors to assist steering without hydraulic components. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of efficiency, weight, and responsiveness, catering to different vehicle requirements and consumer preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
