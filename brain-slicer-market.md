<p><p>The Brain Slicer market is categorized into two main types based on anatomical regions: Coronal Suture and Sagittal Suture. The Coronal Suture type focuses on slicing techniques that align with the frontal and parietal bones, often used in surgeries involving anterior brain structures. Conversely, the Sagittal Suture type pertains to procedures that slice along the midline of the skull, affecting the left and right hemispheres. Both types are essential in neurosurgical procedures, enhancing precision and outcomes in brain therapies.</p></p>
