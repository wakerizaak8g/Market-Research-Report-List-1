<p><strong>The Blown Optical Fiber Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulically Powered</li><li>Pneumatically Powered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blown optical fiber systems are key in telecommunications, facilitating the installation and maintenance of fiber optic cables. The market is divided into two main types: hydraulically powered and pneumatically powered systems. Hydraulically powered systems utilize fluid pressure to propel the fiber through pre-installed ducts, offering high precision and speed. Pneumatically powered systems, on the other hand, use compressed air to achieve similar results, providing flexibility and easier handling. Both types enhance operational efficiency and reduce installation time, catering to diverse network demands.</p></p>
