<p><strong>The EUV Mask-related Inspection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electron Beam Inspection System</li><li>Actinic Systems for EUV Mask Inspection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EUV mask inspection systems market primarily includes two types: Electron Beam Inspection Systems and Actinic Systems. Electron Beam Inspection Systems utilize focused electron beams to detect defects on EUV masks, achieving high resolution and sensitivity for critical applications. In contrast, Actinic Systems operate using light sources that mimic the EUV wavelengths, enabling inspections under conditions similar to the actual lithography process. Both types are crucial for ensuring the quality and reliability of EUV masks in advanced semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
