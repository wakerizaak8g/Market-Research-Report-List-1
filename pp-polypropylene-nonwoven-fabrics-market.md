<p><p>The PP (Polypropylene) nonwoven fabrics market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Spunbonded**: Manufactured by spinning polypropylene filaments, these fabrics are known for their strength and durability, making them ideal for applications like hygiene products and automotive components. </p><p>2. **Staples**: Composed of short fibers, staple nonwovens offer excellent softness and absorbency, suited for items like wipes and filters. </p><p>3. **Composite**: These fabrics combine different technologies, enhancing performance for specialized applications. </p><p>4. **Meltblown**: Created by melting and blowing polypropylene fibers, meltblown fabrics provide fine filtration and are commonly used in medical and protective gear.</p></p>
