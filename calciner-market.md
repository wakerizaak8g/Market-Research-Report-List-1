<p><strong>The Calciner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Directly Heated Calciners</li><li>Indirectly Heated Calciners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The calciner market comprises two primary types: directly heated and indirectly heated calciners. Directly heated calciners utilize flames or hot gases to directly heat materials, enabling rapid temperature increases for efficient processing. In contrast, indirectly heated calciners employ a heat exchange system that transfers warmth without direct contact, providing better temperature control and reduced contamination risk. Both types serve various industries, including metallurgy, ceramics, and chemicals, catering to specific processing needs and material sensitivities.</p></p>
