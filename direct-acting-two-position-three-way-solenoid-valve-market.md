<p><p>The Direct-acting Two-position Three-way Solenoid Valve market is segmented into two main types: Normally Closed (NC) and Normally Open (NO). Normally Closed valves remain in a closed state when de-energized, allowing flow only when activated, making them suitable for applications requiring flow control safety. In contrast, Normally Open valves allow flow when de-energized and close upon activation, ideal for systems needing immediate flow disengagement. Each type serves distinct operational needs across various industries, influencing selection based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
