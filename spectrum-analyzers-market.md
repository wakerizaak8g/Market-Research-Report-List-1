<p><strong>The Spectrum Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Swept-Tuned</li><li>Vector Signal</li><li>Real-Time</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spectrum analyzers market is categorized into three main types: swept-tuned, vector signal, and real-time analyzers. Swept-tuned analyzers measure signal frequencies sequentially over time, providing high sensitivity and resolution. Vector signal analyzers assess both amplitude and phase characteristics, crucial for complex modulation formats, offering insights into signal quality. Real-time analyzers monitor signals continuously, enabling the detection of transient events and rare signals, making them ideal for dynamic environments. Each type serves distinct applications across telecommunications, aerospace, and research sectors.</p></p>
