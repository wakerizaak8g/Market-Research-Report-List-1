<p><ul><li>Spiral Weld Pipe</li><li>Electric Resistance Weld</li><li>Double Submerged Arc Weld</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cold rolled plate market encompasses various types of welded products, including spiral weld pipe, electric resistance weld (ERW) pipe, and double submerged arc weld (DSAW) pipe. Spiral weld pipes are produced by spirally welding a flat strip into a tubular form, ideal for large-diameter applications. ERW pipes utilize electric resistance to weld edges together, suitable for structural purposes. DSAW pipes involve submerged arc welding for thicker materials, providing strength and reliability. Each type caters to specific industries and applications.</p></p>
