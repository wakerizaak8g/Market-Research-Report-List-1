<p><p>The wastewater tanks market is categorized into three primary types: bolted steel tanks, welded steel tanks, and concrete tanks. Bolted steel tanks are prefabricated, allowing for easier transportation and assembly, making them ideal for temporary or modular installations. Welded steel tanks are constructed on-site with continuous welds, providing robust structural integrity for permanent applications. Concrete tanks, often used for larger capacity needs, offer durability and resistance to environmental factors, making them suitable for long-term wastewater management solutions.</p></p>
