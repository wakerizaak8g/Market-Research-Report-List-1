<p><p>The latex Foley catheters market is categorized into three main types: 2-way, 3-way, and 4-way Foley catheters. </p><p>2-way Foley catheters are designed for drainage with one lumen for urine flow and another for inflation of the balloon. </p><p>3-way Foley catheters add an additional lumen for continuous irrigation, often used in post-surgical settings. </p><p>4-way Foley catheters incorporate two lumens for irrigation and dual drainage, providing enhanced management of urinary issues. Each type serves specific medical needs and applications.</p></p>
