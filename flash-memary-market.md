<p><ul><li>NOR Flash</li><li>NAND Flash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flash memory market is primarily divided into two types: NOR Flash and NAND Flash. NOR Flash provides fast read speeds and random access, making it ideal for firmware and code storage in devices like smartphones and embedded systems. In contrast, NAND Flash offers high density and lower cost per bit, making it suitable for data storage in consumer electronics, SSDs, and memory cards. Both types cater to distinct applications, with NAND dominating the market due to its larger capacity and affordability.</p></p>
